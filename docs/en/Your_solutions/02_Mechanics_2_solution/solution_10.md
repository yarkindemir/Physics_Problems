## 10. Force Field and Power

Given:

$$
x = 5t^2 - t,\quad y = 2t^3,\quad z = -3t + 2
$$

Mass:

$$
m = 0.5\ \text{kg}
$$

Find:

- velocity
- momentum
- acceleration
- force
- power

---

### Step 1: Velocity

Velocity is the derivative of position:

$$
\vec v = \left(\frac{dx}{dt},\frac{dy}{dt},\frac{dz}{dt}\right)
$$

$$
\vec v = (10t-1,\ 6t^2,\ -3)
$$

---

### Step 2: Momentum

$$
\vec p = m\vec v
$$

$$
\vec p = 0.5(10t-1,\ 6t^2,\ -3)
$$

$$
\vec p = (5t-0.5,\ 3t^2,\ -1.5)
$$

---

### Step 3: Acceleration

Acceleration is the derivative of velocity:

$$
\vec a = (10,\ 12t,\ 0)
$$

---

### Step 4: Force

Using Newton’s second law:

$$
\vec F = m\vec a
$$

$$
\vec F = 0.5(10,\ 12t,\ 0)
$$

$$
\vec F = (5,\ 6t,\ 0)
$$

---

### Step 5: Power

Power is:

$$
P = \vec F \cdot \vec v
$$

$$
P = (5,\ 6t,\ 0)\cdot(10t-1,\ 6t^2,\ -3)
$$

$$
P = 5(10t-1) + 6t(6t^2)
$$

$$
P = 50t - 5 + 36t^3
$$

---

## Final Answers

$$
\vec v = (10t-1,\ 6t^2,\ -3)
$$

$$
\vec p = (5t-0.5,\ 3t^2,\ -1.5)
$$

$$
\vec a = (10,\ 12t,\ 0)
$$

$$
\vec F = (5,\ 6t,\ 0)
$$

$$
P = 50t - 5 + 36t^3
$$
