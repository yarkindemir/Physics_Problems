A rectangle is under the curve $y = 3 - x^2$ in the first quadrant. What are the dimensions of the rectangle with the maximum area?

Let width be $x$ and height be $y = 3 - x^2$.

Area:
$A(x) = x(3 - x^2) = 3x - x^3$

Maximum occurs at:
$x = 1$

Then:
$y = 3 - 1^2 = 2$

Answer:
Dimensions are $1 \times 2$
