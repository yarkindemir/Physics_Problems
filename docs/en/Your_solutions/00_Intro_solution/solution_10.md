Determine the final position of an ant that starts at the origin and moves according to the following pattern: 1 m east, 1/2 m north, 1/3 m west, 1/4 m south, 1/5 m east, and so on.

Horizontal position:
$x = 1 - \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{5} - \frac{1}{7} + \cdots = \frac{\pi}{4}$

Vertical position:
$y = \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} - \frac{1}{8} + \cdots = \frac{\ln 2}{2}$

Final position:
$\left(\frac{\pi}{4}, \frac{\ln 2}{2}\right)$

Approximate position:
$(0.785,\ 0.347)$
